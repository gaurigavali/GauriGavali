   Wallpaper patterns are more than just eye candy, they have psychological effects on people and can even alter the energy in a space.
 Studies in color psychology, design theory, and environmental psychology have all shown that certain visual patterns and combinations can trigger feelings 
of calmness, energy, creativity, or nostalgia.In fact, research has shown that looking at a specific pattern of wallpaper could also help our brain put
 together puzzle pieces we may not have previously known to be a whole (Can’t wait for that study to be published!).We all know from experience that 
different patterns create different vibes, moods, and energies in a space. For example, soft organic shapes will usually create a feeling of calm, while 
stronger more structured geometric patterns can feel more confident, bold, and sturdy.Unpacking what your wallpaper pattern says about your style, 
personality, and mindset can help you choose wallpaper patterns that resonate with you and align with your emotional needs as well as your personality.
So let’s jump right into our study of popular wallpaper patterns and their psychology to see what your wallpaper choice says about you!
